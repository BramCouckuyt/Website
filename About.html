Our story

Born out of love, built for every family

How it all started

When my son René was born, I was overwhelmed by how beautiful everything was — and how quickly she outgrew it all. A Bonpoint coat worn twice. A Jacadi dress that still had its tags. Tiny shoes that never even touched the ground.

I started to realise that buying everything brand new just didn't make sense anymore. Not for my wallet, and not for the planet. But I still wanted Emma to wear beautiful things. So I started looking for a better way.

That's how Little Luxe was born. What started as a small collection of Emma's outgrown pieces quickly grew into something much bigger — a community of parents who believe that quality clothing deserves a second life.

Made with love

Every item is personally selected and checked. If I wouldn't dress René in it, it doesn't make it into the shop.

Quality that lasts

Designer baby clothing is made to last more than one child. We make sure it gets the chance to do exactly that.

Passed on with care

Each piece comes with its full history — brand, condition and size — so you know exactly what you're getting for your little one.

Thank you for being here

Whether you're here to find something beautiful for your baby, or to give a beloved piece a new home — you're part of something special. Little Luxe exists because of parents like you.
